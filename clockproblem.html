Clock Formulas

1. Angle Between Hour and Minute Hands

  • Angle = |11/2 × M - 30 × H|

2. Angle Between Hands at ‘h’ Hours and ‘m’ Minutes

  • Angle = |30H - 5.5M|

3. Relative Speed of Minute and Hour Hands

  • Relative speed = 5.5° per minute

4. When Hands are at Right Angle (90°)

  • Time = 2/11 hours before/after hour

5. When Hands are in a Straight Line (180°)

  • Time = 6/11 hours before/after hour

6. Coincidence of Hands (Overlapping)

  • Time between coincidences = 12/11 hours = 65 5/11 minutes

7. First Overlap After 12:00

  • First overlap = 1 × 12/11 ≈ 1:05.45

8. Number of Times Hands Coincide in 12 Hours

  • 11 times

9. Hands are at Right Angles How Many Times a Day?

  • 22 times in 12 hours → 44 times a day

10. Hands are in Straight Line How Many Times a Day?

  • 22 times in 12 hours → 44 times a day

11. Hands are Opposite Each Other

  • Occurs every 65.45 minutes (like coincidence, but at 180°)

12. Hour Hand Movement per Minute

  • 0.5° per minute

13. Minute Hand Movement per Minute

  • 6° per minute

14. Gain of Minute Hand Over Hour Hand

  • 5.5° per minute (6 - 0.5)

15. Time for Hands to be at Right Angle

  • M = |60H ± 180| / 5.5

16. Shortest Angle Between Hands

  • θ = min(Angle, 360 - Angle)

17. Speed Ratio of Minute to Hour Hand

  • 6 / 0.5 = 12:1

18. Hands Overlap at:

  • H × 60 / 11 minutes past H o’clock

19. Time Between Two Right Angles

  • 65 5/11 ÷ 2 = 32 8/11 minutes

20. Formula for ‘x’ Minutes Past ‘h’ Hour When Hands Coincide

  • x = 60H / 11
